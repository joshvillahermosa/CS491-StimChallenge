Who we are...

We are a group of fellow students who know the struggles of paying for higher education in today's economy. We strongly believe that it is much better to give than to receive; and what better gift can be bestowed than that of knownledge, opportunity, prestige and sense of accomplishment.

At "name" we provide current and potential students with the opportunity to encourage sponsors in investing in their education and future careers. Our mission statement is to show off students from their best possible side and to secure them a future.

How it works...

Our platform is an easy and simple solution to evergrowing tuition costs and student expenses. By simply creating a profile, students can begin gathering funds for their college expenses in a fun and easy way. Whether it is getting your friends and family involved or outside sponsors, the key to success lies in your hands. After creating your profile any and all funds you gather will be securely stored until you have reached your goal. You set your own financial goals and can end your fundraiser at any given time. All pledges will be directly transferred to the school of your choosing as part of or a complete tuition. Whether it is just a couple of bucks you need for books or a full tuition, our platform provides you with these opportunities. In the event that a student cannot attend the college of their choosing, cannot continue going to college for any reason or simply no longer requires the aid of the fundrasier, all gathered funds will be returned to the people who have pledged them. This is a safe, fast and fun way to shape your future. The only limit is your creativity and imagination.
